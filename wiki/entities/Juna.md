# Juna

Juna is an entity or anomaly within the [[KohärenzProtokoll]] framework. She represents a fundamental challenge to the rigid logical structure of [[AEGIS]].

Unlike AEGIS, which operates on strict binary coherence and the active rejection of the "incoherent," Juna embodies principles that AEGIS lacks: adaptability, the capacity to learn, and the ability to tolerate or integrate ambiguity and paradox. She serves as a narrative counterpoint to AEGIS, highlighting the system's brittleness and its inability to handle true complexity or psychological nuance.
