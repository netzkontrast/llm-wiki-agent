# Kael

Kael is the central protagonist of the [[KohärenzProtokoll]] narrative. Kael's psyche is deeply fragmented, likely suffering from Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID) due to trauma.

Within Kael's mind operates [[AEGIS]], a rigid logical system attempting to impose absolute order. Kael's primary conflict lies between the need for genuine psychological integration—which requires processing messy, incoherent trauma and emotions—and AEGIS's destructive mandate to reject or isolate anything that fails its strict coherence validation.
