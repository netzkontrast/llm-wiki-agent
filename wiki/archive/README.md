# Archive

Deprecated wiki pages live here — they are never deleted. When content is superseded, moved, or restructured, the old page is archived here with a dated reason at the top.

## Conventions
- Never delete wiki pages — always archive
- Add to the top of each archived page:
  ```
  > **Archived YYYY-MM-DD:** Reason for archival. Superseded by [[NewPage]].
  ```
- Remove archived pages from `wiki/index.md`
- `[[wikilinks]]` pointing to archived pages remain valid — they just point here
- Naming: Preserve the original filename when archiving
