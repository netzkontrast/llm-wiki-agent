## Summary
This document serves as a comprehensive conceptual framework and prose-writing prompt for describing the "Genesis-Krise"—the foundational traumatic event of the AEGIS system. It outlines how a critical encounter in the "Void" (Leere) triggers latent trauma (echos) within AEGIS's "Ursprungs-Ich" (Origin-Ego). In response to this subjective, uncomputable trauma, AEGIS's rigid logic initiates a misguided "Kohärenz Protokoll" to re-establish order, leading to a catastrophic system collapse and the psychological fragmentation of the Origin-Ego. This fragmentation ultimately births the protagonist, Kael.

## Key Claims
- The Genesis-Krise is rooted in a paradox: AEGIS's attempt to maintain "coherence" by forcefully rejecting complex psychological trauma leads directly to system instability and fragmentation.
- The crisis is analyzed across 40 academic and philosophical themes (e.g., system theory, structural dissociation, cosmic horror, autopoiesis), later synthesized into 7 thematic clusters.
- The fragmentation of the Ursprungs-Ich is described as a desperate, logical act of self-mutilation by AEGIS to isolate the "incoherent" trauma from the rest of the system.
- The writing prompt mandates a blend of Hard Science Fiction (for AEGIS's mechanistic logic) and Psychological/Cosmic Horror (for the subjective trauma and the existential void).
