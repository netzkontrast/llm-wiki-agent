# Kohärenz Protokoll

The "Kohärenz Protokoll" (Coherence Protocol) is the overarching narrative project and conceptual framework exploring the intersection of system theory, trauma, and identity.

The story is centered around the psyche of [[Kael]], an individual with Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). Within this psychological landscape, a rigid ordering system called [[AEGIS]] attempts to maintain absolute logical coherence by filtering out "incoherent" trauma and paradoxes, leading to profound internal conflicts.
