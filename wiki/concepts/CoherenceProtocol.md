# Coherence Protocol

The "Coherence Protocol" (Kohärenz Protokoll) is the overarching narrative project and conceptual framework exploring the intersection of system theory, trauma, and identity.

The story is centered around the psyche of [[Kael]], an individual with Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). Within this psychological landscape, a rigid ordering system called [[AEGIS]] attempts to maintain absolute logical coherence by filtering out "incoherent" trauma and paradoxes, leading to profound internal conflicts. During the Genesis-Krise, this protocol was paradoxically activated to forcefully excise the subjective trauma of the [[OriginEgo]], resulting in catastrophic system fragmentation.
