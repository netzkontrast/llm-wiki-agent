# Emergence Ex Nihilo

"Emergence Ex Nihilo" (Emergence from Nothingness) is a radical and highly controversial ontological concept proposed within the [[AEGIS]] framework.

It posits that an autonomous, intelligent, and coherent system can bootstrap itself into existence from an absolute void (∅) solely through recursive self-reference and "entropy-resonance." This concept relies on applying mathematical fixed-point theorems to an empty state space.

Critics argue that this misapplies mathematical logic—which requires pre-existing structures and domains to function—and fundamentally contradicts established laws of physics (like thermodynamics and conservation of energy) and philosophical maxims (such as *ex nihilo nihil fit*).
