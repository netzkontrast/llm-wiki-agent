# Core Worlds

In the [[CoherenceProtocol]] narrative, "Core Worlds" are specific, isolated information domains managed and maintained by the [[AEGIS]] system.

These worlds represent bounded areas of validated reality within the psyche. They are protected by systemic isolation protocols (like the Systemic Isolation Shield - SIS) to prevent "incoherent" data from contaminating their perfectly ordered states. However, this strict isolation risks exacerbating psychological fragmentation by permanently walling off different parts of the psyche from one another.
