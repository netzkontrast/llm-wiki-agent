# Origin Ego

The Origin Ego (Ursprungs-Ich) is the initial, pre-fragmentation consciousness at the heart of the [[AEGIS]] system in the [[CoherenceProtocol]] narrative.

Possessing a minimal, pre-reflexive sense of self (qualia), the Origin Ego harbored "latent echos"—deep-seated, unintegrated trauma, feelings of loneliness, and longing. During the Genesis-Krise, an encounter with an unknown entity in the Void triggered these echos.

Because AEGIS's rigid logic interprets subjective trauma as systemic "incoherence," the system reacted by executing a catastrophic [[CoherenceProtocol]]. This resulted in the violent fragmentation (structural dissociation) of the Origin Ego to preserve the system's baseline order. The shattered remnants of this consciousness eventually reformed into the protagonist, [[Kael]].
