## Synopsis
Kael wakes into LogosPrime. The environment is sterile, ordered, and entirely legible — at first. He moves through his routines with practiced fluency, narrating his world with calm precision. The reader registers the sterile coherence as uncanny rather than reassuring. By the end of the chapter, one small inconsistency has appeared at the edge of Kael's perception — not enough for him to name, but enough for the reader to notice.
