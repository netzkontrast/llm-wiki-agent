## Description
The physical or symbolic manifestation of Kael's central trauma (e.g., "Betrayal") within the emotional plane of KW2. It represents the repressed, painful core of his psychological wound.

## Characteristics
- **Plane:** KW2 (Resonanz-Landschaft / Mnemosyne)
- **Aesthetic:** Decaying structure (ruined temple, overgrown monument), dark and heavy colors (rust red, dark gray) contrasting with a painfully bright central element.
- **Atmosphere:** Oppressive, heavy, melancholic. Smells of dust and decay, sensations of cold drafts.
- **Narrative Function:** Serves as the central confrontation point in KW2 where Kael must face his past and repressed emotions. The environment actively draws him in.
