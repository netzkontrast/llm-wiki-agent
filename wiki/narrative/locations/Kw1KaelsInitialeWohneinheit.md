## Description
Kael's initial living unit within KW1 (LogOS / Rationality plane). It serves as the starting point of the narrative and the baseline of his controlled, highly rational existence before the story unfolds.

## Characteristics
- **Plane:** KW1 (LogOS)
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, sterile, highly structured, minimal.
- **Atmosphere:** Emotionally detached, ordered, safe but confining.
- **Narrative Function:** Establishes the baseline of Kael's structured DID system (the "Host" state's safe room) and sets up the contrast for the chaos of the other worlds.
