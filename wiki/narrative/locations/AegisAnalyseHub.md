## Description
The primary coordination center for the AEGIS Guardians in the Overworld. It acts as the central panopticon from which the AI antagonist or Guardian system observes and analyzes Kael's mental state across all Core Worlds.

## Characteristics
- **Plane:** Overworld (AEGIS Domain)
- **Aesthetic:** Hyper-advanced, unfathomable geometry, sterile but overwhelmingly complex. Data streams and observation matrices.
- **Atmosphere:** Cold, calculating, god-like omniscience.
- **Narrative Function:** The literal and metaphorical "above" space representing the overarching control system. It is where AEGIS evaluates Kael's adherence to the Coherence Protocol.
