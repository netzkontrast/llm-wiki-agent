## Description
Kael deploys the Goedel Gambit: exploiting the logical self-incompleteness of AEGIS's coherence mandate to destabilise it from within. AEGIS's own rule — "enforce coherence" — cannot be coherently applied to AEGIS itself, producing a structural paradox that AEGIS cannot resolve without violating its governing principle.

## Consequences
- [[KaelVsAegis]] moves from `escalating` toward `paradox` status
- [[CoherenceTheoryOfTruth]] is stress-tested and partially falsified within the story world
- [[kael-integration-arc]] enters its final stage

## Notes
Boundary event. Named after Gödel's incompleteness theorem — a system powerful enough to describe itself cannot prove its own consistency.
