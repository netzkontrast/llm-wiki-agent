# Overview

*This page is maintained by the LLM. It is updated on every ingest to reflect the current synthesis across all sources.*

The foundation of the wiki's narrative theory begins with a structured approach to storytelling. The [[40-chapter-plot-module]] outlines a universal, genre-agnostic 40-scene framework inspired by screenwriting. By dividing a novel into four 10-chapter acts (Act 1, 2A, 2B, 3), it maps out an interconnected [[CharacterArc]]—moving from an initial fatal flaw, through false victories and dark moments, toward an ultimate transformation and resolution. This standardized [[NovelStructure]] aims to help writers construct a well-paced 60,000–120,000 word manuscript.
