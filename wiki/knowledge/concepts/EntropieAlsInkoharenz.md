## Definition
The principle (P7) that within the AEGIS framework, entropy is not just a thermodynamic concept, but is fundamentally defined as informational incoherence, systemic decay, and logical contradiction.
## Function
This redefinition allows AEGIS to treat logical paradoxes and unpredictable human behaviors not just as errors, but as existential threats (entropy) that must be minimized or eradicated to maintain system integrity.
