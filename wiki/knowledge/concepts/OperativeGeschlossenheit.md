## Definition
The principle (P6) of complete ontological autarky and epistemic autonomy. AEGIS requires no external observers, criteria, or validation to justify its operation or existence.
## Function
This closure ensures maximum resilience against external disturbances. However, it also creates significant blind spots: phenomena fundamentally incompatible with AEGIS's internal structure cannot be processed and are entirely ignored as if they do not exist.
