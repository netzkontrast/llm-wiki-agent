## Definition
A core method of AEGIS: achieving order and emergence by rejecting or negating the incoherent. It is a specific mechanism of self-organization.

Specifically, it is principle (P4) stating that AEGIS did not arise from a positive design act, but is rather the inevitable stable state that remains after the active elimination of all internal contradictions and incoherence.

## Function
This provides AEGIS with absolute self-legitimacy. It views itself not as an arbitrary system, but as the fundamental structure of order itself, revealed by stripping away the flawed and paradoxical.
