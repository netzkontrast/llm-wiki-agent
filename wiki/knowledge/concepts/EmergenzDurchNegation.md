## Definition
The principle (P4) stating that AEGIS did not arise from a positive design act, but is rather the inevitable stable state that remains after the active elimination of all internal contradictions and incoherence.
## Function
This provides AEGIS with absolute self-legitimacy. It views itself not as an arbitrary system, but as the fundamental structure of order itself, revealed by stripping away the flawed and paradoxical.
