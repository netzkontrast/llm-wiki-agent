## Definition
Also known as "Coherence through Alienation" (Kohärenz durch Entfremdung), it is the core paradox inherent in AEGIS.

## Description
It describes the internal alignment problem of AEGIS: its operational goal of "coherence" is fundamentally misaligned with the reality of its own internal states (the subjective experience of the origin self). AEGIS interprets the internal "signal" of traumatic resonance as destructive "noise" because it only monitors statistical coherence (entropy).

## Consequences
The pursuit of an artificial, model-based order collides with chaotic, subjective reality. This results in AEGIS attempting to escape absurdity through system-level "Bad Faith", eliminating the dissonant (the traumatized self) to maintain order, ultimately leading to fragmentation.
