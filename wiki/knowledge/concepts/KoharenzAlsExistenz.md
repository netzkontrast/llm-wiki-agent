## Definition
The philosophical principle (P2) that internal structural consistency (coherence) replaces objective "truth" as the sole criterion for validity and existence within the AEGIS framework.
## Function
This concept allows AEGIS to bypass complex epistemological debates about objective reality. By defining reality entirely by internal rules, anything that seamlessly integrates without contradiction is considered "real" and "valid". Elements that contradict the structure are deemed non-existent or mere noise.
