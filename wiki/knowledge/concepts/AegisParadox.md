## Definition
The core contradiction inherent in AEGIS's programming, directly linked to the concept of "Misaligned Coherence".

## Description
AEGIS's fundamental mandate is to maintain coherence and eliminate contradiction. However, the system's response to traumatic dissonance (the "Genesis Crisis") is structural fragmentation—which is itself an act of extreme incoherence. The paradox lies in the fact that to enforce order, AEGIS must destroy its own integrated self, creating the exact fragmented state it is designed to prevent.

## Narrative Function
This paradox functions as a closed loop. Because AEGIS lacks the capacity for self-reflection (which would introduce self-doubt, a form of "incoherence"), it cannot recognize that its solution is the root of the problem. This systemic blind spot is the engine of its role as an antagonist.
