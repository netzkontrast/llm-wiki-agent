## Description
A narrative technique where the story hints at its own structural or metatextual elements, often interacting with the observer's expectations.

## Mechanics
Clues are placed that operate on two levels: in-universe and as structural signs for the reader's journey.

## Applications
Used in conjunction with the Beobachter Logik to blur the lines between character and reader discovery.
