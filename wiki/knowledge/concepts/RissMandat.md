## Definition
The Riss-Mandat is a mandate or event that supposedly resulted in Kael having 13 Alters. However, recordings from the Integrity Guardian state that the Riss-Mandat was never fully executed, and Kael only has 11 Alters.

## Mechanism
It is associated with the fragmentation or splitting of the mind into alters.

## Notes
There is a contradiction in the text regarding whether this mandate was actually executed.
