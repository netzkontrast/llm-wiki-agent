## Definition
The core worldbuilding principle in "Kohärenz Protokoll" where the physical environments (the four Core Worlds / Kern-Welten) act as direct externalizations of the protagonist Kael's psyche, specifically his Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID).

## Narrative Function
The locations are not passive backgrounds but active manifestations of Kael's internal state. Each Core World is linked to a specific psychological domain and governed by an AEGIS Guardian. The design and atmosphere of these landscapes directly reflect the mental health, traumas, and functions of the alters residing there.
