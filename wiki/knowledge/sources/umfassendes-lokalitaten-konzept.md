## Summary
This document serves as a comprehensive, detailed, and atmospheric location concept for the novel "Kohärenz Protokoll". It outlines 31 specific locations crucial for the first part of the novel, functioning as a "World Bible" for the narrative settings. The locations are distributed across the four Core Worlds (KW1-KW4) and the Overworld (AEGIS), deeply tied to the psychological states of the DID protagonist, Kael.

## Key Claims
- The four Core Worlds (KW1-KW4) function as externalizations of Kael's psyche, not merely as passive backgrounds.
- Each reality level is guarded by a specific AEGIS Guardian (LogOS, Mnemosyne, Cerberus, Kairos/Sophia).
- Environmental storytelling is paramount; the landscape itself reflects the inner state of the protagonist.
- 31 distinct locations exist, serving various narrative and psychological functions (e.g., Transit corridors, Trauma loci, Resonance landscapes).
