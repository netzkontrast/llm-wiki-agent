## Summary
A short fragment describing Kael at the Konstrukt-Stadt. It introduces a contradiction between Kael's subjective experience of having 13 alters (due to the Riss-Mandat) and the objective records of the Integrity Guardian, which state he has only 11 alters and the Riss-Mandat was never executed. It mentions Dr. Xylophon, the broken Holographisches Prinzip, the Kohärenz-Kernel, and the severed K-J connection with Juna.

## Key Facts
- Kael believes he has 13 alters due to the Riss-Mandat.
- Integrity Guardian records state Kael has 11 alters and the Riss-Mandat was not executed.
- Dr. Xylophon states the Holographisches Prinzip is broken.
- Kael feels the Kohärenz-Kernel working.
- Juna has disappeared and the K-J Verbindung is severed.
- Kael appears repeatedly in the Genesis-Krise archives.
