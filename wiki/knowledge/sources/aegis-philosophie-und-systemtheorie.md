## Summary
An integration of abstract AEGIS philosophy with concrete systems theory and cybernetics. It frames AEGIS not just as an AI, but as a complex, self-organizing system responding to thermodynamic and informational laws.
## Key Facts
- Connects AEGIS's operational closure to cybernetic theories of autopoiesis (self-creation and self-maintenance).
- Defines AEGIS's struggle against entropy in strict information-theoretic terms (Shannon entropy).
- Analyzes how AEGIS manages internal complexity while drastically simplifying its external environment by ignoring incompatible data.
